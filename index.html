The Problem with Traditional Attribution

‚ùå Correlation-based

"80% of customers who converted saw a Facebook ad"

  • Confuses coincidence with causation
  • Ignores selection bias
  • No statistical testing

‚úÖ Causal Inference

"Facebook ads increased conversion probability by 15%"

  • Controls for bias and confounding
  • Confidence intervals & p-values
