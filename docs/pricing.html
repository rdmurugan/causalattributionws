Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

If you exceed your monthly touchpoint or API call limit, we'll automatically upgrade you to the next tier for that month. You'll receive a notification before any upgrade occurs.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing comes with a 15% discount. Contact sales for annual pricing details.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required to start. Contact us at durai@infinidatum.net to begin.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, AmEx), ACH transfers, and wire transfers for annual plans.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer a 25% discount for qualified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact sales for details.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your first month's payment in full.
